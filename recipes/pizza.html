Pizza

Originating from Italy, pizza has become a global phenomenon. The basic ingredients are combined in endless ways, making it one of the most popular foods in the world

Ingredients
    Dough,Tomato sauce,Mozzarella
Steps
  • Combine flour, water, yeast, and salt in a bowl. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic. Let the dough rise in a warm place until doubled in size.
  • Roll out the dough into a desired shape. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough. Add your favorite toppings, such as cheese, vegetables, and meats.
  • Preheat your oven to a high temperature. Place the pizza on a baking stone or pizza pan. Bake until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
